1. Returns and Refunds & Return Due To Change of Mind
Awesomepencilcases.com will happily accept returns due to change of mind as long as the
request is received by us within 30 days of you receiving the item.
The item must be returned to us in the original packaging, unopened, and unused.
The return shipping costs will be paid by the customer. Once we receive the
package, the refund will be processed and you will receive the full value of the product as a refund.
